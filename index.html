What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is when computers learn to think and solve problems like super-smart helpers.

AI learns from information, spots patterns, and helps people make better decisions faster.

In India, AI is helping farmers, doctors, students, entrepreneurs, and communities in amazing ways.

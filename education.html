AI in Education

AI is helping students learn at their own pace, helping teachers, and opening new skill pathways for India's future.

These real programs show how learning apps, government platforms, and school labs are making education more exciting and more reachable.

What it does: National digital learning platform with interactive content in 36 Indian languages.

For Whom: Students from pre-primary to Grade 12 and teachers across India.

Cool Results: Millions of learners access textbooks, quizzes, and videos for free.

AI for Environment

AI is helping India study the weather, protect forests and wildlife, warn people about floods, and monitor cleaner air and water.

These real programs show how data, satellites, and smart forecasting can help communities prepare for climate and environmental challenges.

Gramin Krishi Mausam Sewa

What it does: Sends AI-enhanced weather advisories specific to each district by SMS and apps.

For Whom: Farmers in all districts of India.

Cool Results: Location-specific crop guidance based on weather forecasts.

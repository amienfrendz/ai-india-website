AI in Finance

AI is helping families open bank accounts, get benefits safely, make digital payments, and access loans, insurance, and savings tools.

These real financial inclusion programs show how digital systems can make money services safer, faster, and easier for everyone.

Pan-India (Central Government)

PM Jan Dhan Yojana + AI

What it does: Universal banking for unbanked Indians with AI helping fraud detection and KYC.

For Whom: Rural and low-income families with no bank accounts.

Cool Results: 57 crore+ accounts opened and 67% are in rural areas.
